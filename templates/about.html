Our Story

The Village is a co-working space served with motivation and innovation that is focused on Community building for Freelancers, Startups, and Enterprises, who have a strong desire for Productivity. Our success story is hinged on the growth and success story of our clients.

In addition to providing workspace, the village serves as a platform for hosting events relevant to professionals and entrepreneurs by partnering with leading organizations in Nigeria. The village is not just a home base but a community where entrepreneurs can flourish.

Mission

To create a conducive working environment for seamless co-working experience to foster creativity, innovation, and networking, focused on achieving productivity and success.

Vision

Foster a community that bolsters creative and innovative partnerships.

Core Values

  • ✓ Productivity
  • ✓ Community
  • ✓ Togetherness and Collaboration
  • ✓ Diversity and Sustainability

Startups

We are a startup so we know the challenges faced by startups. Access to accommodation goes beyond an offer of meeting and work rooms but as one of us, you automatically enjoy our community of mentors and developers around.

Small and Medium Enterprises

Small teams and companies make up a growing part of the co-working space market. You would benefit from our events and networking opportunities to build connections with possible clients, investors and business professionals.

Enterprise

We got you covered in your business meetings, conferences, or expansion of office. You also stand to enjoy our community of innovators and business professionals.
